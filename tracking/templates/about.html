Our Beginning

Crafting Memories Through Scents

Founded in 2010, Perfume Store began as a small boutique in Surat, Gujarat, with a passion for bringing the world's finest fragrances to our customers. What started as a modest shop with just 50 fragrances has blossomed into one of India's premier online perfume destinations.

Our founder, Rajesh Mehta, a certified perfumer with over 20 years of experience in the fragrance industry, envisioned a place where scent enthusiasts could discover both classic and contemporary fragrances with expert guidance.

"A fragrance should tell your story before you speak a word."
- Rajesh Mehta, Founder

Our Mission

To curate the world's finest fragrances and make them accessible to everyone, while educating our customers about the art and science of perfumery.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted fragrance destination, known for our expertise, authenticity, and exceptional customer service.

Our Promise

100% authentic perfumes, expert recommendations, and a seamless shopping experience from discovery to delivery.
